Safe Multispeciality Hospital

Safe multispeciality Hospital , Guntur has been a well recognized and leading healthcare provider in the city for decades and has treated and nurtured patients across generations. A vision to create a world-class integrated healthcare delivery system in India, entailing the finest medical skills combined with compassionate care gave birth to Safe Hospitals in July 2010. When a few well-known, dedicated Oncologists with zeal to provide exceptional patient care came together, they realized the lack of comprehensive care for cancer patients in India.
